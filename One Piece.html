One Piece👒


One Piece is a manga as well as an anime which was written by a japanese writer Echiro Oda. The first manga chapter of One Piece was published on 1997 and the first anime episode was published on 1999. Currently One Piece has 1010 manga chapter and 970 anime episodes. Its is most sold manga ever. Around 400 million of manga copies have been sold. Echiro Oda is the richest manga artist(200 million $). One Piece is still going with full pace.
